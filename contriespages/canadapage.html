CANADA

Why Study in Canada?

Canada is globally recognized as a leader in education and innovation, making it a top destination for students seeking to further their studies abroad.

Globally Recognized Courses: Wide range of degree and diploma programs.
Affordable Education: Cost is relatively affordable compared to UK/USA.
Safe Living: Ranked as one of the safest countries.
Post-Study Opportunities: Gain up to 3 years of PGWP.

Latest Updates for Canada Study Visas (2025)

Study Permit Cap: 2025 cap reduced by 10%.
Language Requirements: University (IELTS 6.0), College (IELTS 4.0-4.5).

Requirements for Canada Study Visa

  • Valid Passport: Must be up-to-date.
  • Proof of Funds: Cover tuition and living expenses.
  • Academic Documents: Mark sheets and test scores.
